About Me

My name is Peter Ascoli and I am pursuing a Master of Science in Mechanical Engineering at MIT. I received my Bachelor of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering from The Cooper Union. I am most interested in precision machine design, manufacturing, and control, with aspirations to work in the aerospace industry after completing my masters degree. In addition to my passion for engineering, I am also an enthusiast photographer. This website was created to showcase some of my personal and academic projects in engineering and photography. Enjoy!

Please feel free to view my resume and contact me if you are interested.
